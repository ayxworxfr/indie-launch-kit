# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

Thank you for using our application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

When using the App, you may voluntarily provide:

- Account registration information (e.g., username, email address)
- Content data created within the App

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

To provide and improve our services, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: Feature usage frequency, crash logs (only for bug fixing)
- **Network Information**: IP address, network connection type

We do **not** collect: location data, contacts, microphone or camera data (unless explicitly required by a feature and authorized by you).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Collected information is used solely for:

1. **Service Delivery**: Ensuring the App functions properly
2. **Experience Improvement**: Analyzing usage patterns to optimize features
3. **Technical Support**: Diagnosing and fixing technical issues
4. **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse

**We do not sell your personal information to third parties.**

## 3. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption
- We regularly review our security measures to prevent unauthorized access
- Data retention: personal data is deleted within 30 days after account deletion

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App may integrate the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

- Apple App Store / Google Play (app distribution)
- Crash reporting service (for bug fixes only)

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate personal information
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and related data
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw your consent to data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected children's personal information, we will delete it immediately.

## 7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When changes are made, we will notify you within the App. Continued use of the App constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
